Uphill Travel Status

11/20/23: The light is set to RED which means uphill travel is currently CLOSED due to heavy machinery on the mountain as we prepare for opening day. Please help us keep you and our staff safe so that we may start spinning lifts as soon as possible. Thank you for your cooperation. 

View Policy

Snow Report

Welcome to the 2023-24 season.  Stay on open, groomed runs only.  With such a thin snowpack, travel off groomed runs is not recommended.  We have a lot of snowmaking machinery on the mountain, please give all machinery, and employees working them, ample space and respect; so we can work to get as much of mountain open as possible.  We also updated our website to reflect our year to date snowfall, not our season to date.  From Oct 1 to Nov 23 we got 54" of snow.  From Nov 24 (opening day) to now (12-17-23) we've gotten 73" of snow, to bring our season to date total to 127".
