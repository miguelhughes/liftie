New Terrain This Weekend

South Peak opens Saturday

Today was nice and sunny and we are expecting more of the same tomorrow. Temperatures are forecasted to reach highs in the low 30s at the base and low 20s at summit. Snowmaking is still fired up at South Peak and Loon Peak, we are keeping the guns blazing around the clock.

The White Mountain Express Gondola, North Peak Express Quad, Kancamagus 8, Kissin Cousin Double, Seven Brothers Express Quad, and Sarsaparilla Carpet will begin spinning at 9am Friday granting access to 133 acres terrain.

This Saturday will mark the season debut of South Peak with the opening of Cruiser, Lincon Express Quad, Tote Road Quad and Pemi Base Camp.

Please note: Uphill Access is still closed at this time.
