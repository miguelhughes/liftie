Snow Report

Current Conditions

Last Updated: Mar 8, 2024
Report:

The sun is back bringing us a spring like day, with temps hitting 50. Temps dropped overnight which allowed our groomers to get back out after a few nights off. Expect machine groomed and loose granular conditions to start the day. Things should soften up nicely as the day goes on.

Scheduled lifts will be operating from 9am-4pm today. The Tubing and Mountain Adventure Parks will be open 4pm-8pm.
